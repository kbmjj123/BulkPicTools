How It Works

Three steps. No server involved.

Your browser does all the heavy lifting via WebAssembly.

01
Select Files

Drag & drop your images. Supports JPG, PNG, WebP, HEIC, AVIF, GIF and more. No upload wait time.

02
Process Locally

Our WebAssembly engine runs entirely in your browser. Data never leaves your device — ever.

03
Instant Save

Download all processed files immediately, in a ZIP or individually. No server limits, no watermarks.
